# First Tutorial Flow

The game now begins with free movement. A short hint points the player to the Farmer; the Farmer's original introduction only begins after the player presses `E` to talk to him.

1. Talk to the Farmer.
2. Talk to the Farmer's Wife.
3. **Trip 1:** collect and deliver six small fieldstones by hand.
4. **Trip 2:** use the skidsteer to collect and deliver the remaining ten fieldstones.
5. Completing Trip 2 makes the winch attachment arrive.
6. **Trip 3:** install the attachment, then use it to haul the large log into Home Delivery.

The six small stones are the only hand-pickable fieldstones. The existing `obj_rock` fieldstones are large, skidsteer-only material; ten of them complete the 16-stone cabin target during Trip 2.
